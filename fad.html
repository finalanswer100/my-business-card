Top three activities to do at Fadunsin

Farming is our art

The people of Fadunsin practice Farming on daily basis to sustain ourselves and to provide food for the entire community. Infacts,we sell part of our harvests e.g Cassava,Yam,Tomatoes e.t.c to our neigbouring villages and nearby towns to make money.

Sport is our hobby

Football is our general game in the land.Both male and female,young and old derive pleasure in footballing.Due to our tremedous love for football, many of us are now outside there playing for both local and international leagues.This has brought rapid growth to our community.

Politics is Dominance

Our adults from the age 18yrs and above are engaged in politics in order to fight for social amenties such as electricity,pipe borne water supply and etc. in collective effort with our educated people to our community conduceive for us.
